Legacy Mode

It seems that your browser is not supporting some required functionalities or we know about troubles with your browser. That's the reason why you are using the auto tagger in a legacy mode. Basically that's not a problem but it means your are sending the a downsampled version of your image (256 x 256px) to our backend instead of a small feature vector. As an example, the feature vector of the non-legacy mode has a size of 360 Byte while the image in the legacy mode has ~250 kByte.
